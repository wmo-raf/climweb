# ClimWeb Harassment Protection Framework

Policies and Protection Processes for Users and Contributors

---

## Comprehensive Code of Conduct

### Professional Standards for All Users

- **Institutional professionalism:** Appropriate conduct for government meteorological staff and public users
- **Scientific integrity:** Accurate, evidence-based communication in all interactions
- **Respectful communication:** Professional courtesy in comments, feedback, and public interactions
- **Public service ethics:** Maintaining appropriate standards when engaging with community members
- **Educational focus:** Keeping interactions relevant to meteorological and climate information

### Prohibited Behaviors Across All Interaction Types

- **Harassment and intimidation:** Personal attacks, bullying, or threatening behavior in any platform feature
- **Inappropriate content:** Sexual harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language in comments or forms
- **Spam and abuse:** Misuse of contact forms, event registrations, or feedback mechanisms
- **Professional misconduct:** Staff abuse of administrative privileges or inappropriate use of internal messaging
- **Privacy violations:** Sharing personal information without consent through any platform feature

- **Data protection:** Secure handling of participant information
- **Anonymous reporting:** Options for reporting concerning behavior during events

#### Survey Participation and Feedback Collection

- **Content review:** Staff monitoring of all survey responses and feedback
- **Anonymous options:** Protected participation without revealing personal information
- **Response filtering:** Automated detection of inappropriate or abusive content
- **Professional mediation:** Staff oversight of feedback review and response

#### Contact Forms for Public Inquiries

- **Spam filtering:** Automated detection of malicious or inappropriate messages
- **Priority routing:** Urgent safety concerns directed to appropriate staff
- **Response protocols:** Professional handling of all public inquiries
- **Escalation procedures:** Clear processes for concerning communications

### Internal Staff Collaboration Protection

#### Editorial Workflows

- **Role-based permissions:** Controlled access preventing unauthorized content changes
- **Audit trails:** Complete logging of all editorial actions and communications

- **Professional mediation:** Institutional conflict resolution for editorial disputes

#### Internal Messaging

- **Professional use only:** Clear guidelines limiting internal communications to work-related topics
- **Message monitoring:** Administrative oversight capabilities for inappropriate use
- **HR integration:** Direct connection to institutional human resources for workplace issues
- **Privacy controls:** Secure, encrypted internal communications

#### Collaborative Content Creation

- **Version control:** Protection against malicious editing or content sabotage
- **Access logging:** Complete records of who edited what content when
- **Conflict resolution:** Clear procedures for resolving content disagreements
- **Backup systems:** Content recovery capabilities for damaged materials

### Community and Professional Engagement Protection

#### Public Participation in Educational Programs

- **Supervised sessions:** Professional staff oversight of all public educational events

- **Professional boundaries:** Clear guidelines for appropriate public engagement

#### Stakeholder Feedback on Weather Services

#### User-Generated Content (Surveys, Comments, Community Input)

- **Moderation system:** All user-generated content reviewed before publication
- **Content guidelines:** Clear standards for appropriate community contributions
- **User reporting:** Easy mechanisms for reporting inappropriate content
- **Response procedures:** Professional handling of community concerns

#### Professional Networking Among Meteorological Professionals

- **Professional standards:** Code of conduct for inter-agency and professional communications
- **Institutional oversight:** Administrative monitoring of professional networking activities
- **Conflict resolution:** Procedures for addressing professional disputes
- **Resource sharing:** Secure channels for appropriate professional collaboration

## Response and Enforcement Procedures

### Multi-Level Response System

#### Immediate Actions

- **Content removal:** Instant deletion of clearly inappropriate material
- **User blocking:** Temporary restriction of access for problematic users
- **Staff notification:** Immediate alerts to administrators for serious incidents
- **Evidence preservation:** Automatic backup of reported content for investigation

#### Investigation Procedures

- **Professional review:** Institutional staff assessment of reported incidents
- **Fair process:** Opportunity for all parties to provide information
- **External support:** Connection to HR, legal, or law enforcement when needed
- **Documentation:** Complete records maintained for institutional accountability

## Safety and Security of Underage Users

### Mixed Access Environment Protections

#### Public Access with Enhanced Monitoring

**Educational Content Access:**

- **Age-appropriate content:** All public materials suitable for educational environments
- **Supervised browsing:** Encouraging parental or teacher supervision for educational use
- **No registration required:** Minors can access educational content without providing personal information
- **Safe content standards:** Extra review for any content accessible to educational users

**Limited Interaction Capabilities:**

- **Moderated comments:** Enhanced review for any public comments from educational users
- **Educational event focus:** Public events designed for supervised educational participation
- **Group participation:** Event registrations handled through educational institutions
- **No direct messaging:** Minors cannot access internal messaging or direct communication features

### Enhanced Protection Protocols

#### Supervised Educational Engagement

- **Institutional partnerships:** Formal agreements with schools and educational organizations
- **Adult supervision required:** All minor participation must include qualified adult oversight
- **Group settings prioritized:** Educational programs designed for classroom or group participation
- **Professional boundaries:** Clear staff guidelines for appropriate interactions with educational groups

#### Technical Safeguards

- **Enhanced content filtering:** Additional screening for content accessible to minors
- **Session monitoring:** Technical oversight of educational access patterns
- **Limited feature access:** Educational users cannot access advanced interactive features
- **Parental notification:** Information provided to parents/teachers about platform use

### Staff Training for Educational Interactions

#### Professional Development

- **Child protection training:** Required training for staff involved in educational outreach
- **Appropriate boundaries:** Clear guidelines for professional interactions with educational groups
- **Recognition training:** Education on identifying concerning behavior or safety issues
- **Reporting obligations:** Understanding of legal requirements for child protection

## Implementation Across Platform Features

### Feature-Specific Protections

#### Comments and User-Generated Content (Educational Context)

- **Teacher mediation:** Educational submissions reviewed through qualified educators
- **Group submissions:** Individual comments discouraged, group feedback preferred
- **Content appropriateness:** Extra screening for educational-context submissions

#### Contact Forms (Educational Inquiries)

- **Educational context identification:** Special handling for inquiries from educational institutions
- **Parent/teacher mediation:** Direct communications discouraged, institutional contact preferred
- **Professional response:** Educational inquiries handled by qualified institutional staff
- **Follow-up protocols:** Appropriate institutional follow-up procedures for educational requests

- **Legal compliance:** Full adherence to national child protection reporting requirements
- **Documentation protocols:** Proper evidence preservation for any investigations
